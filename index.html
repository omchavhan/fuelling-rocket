Our Services

FUND RAISING ADVISORY

At Fuelling Rockets, we specialize in helping businesses secure funding ranging from INR 1 crore to 100 crores. Whether you are a startup or an established company, we tailor strategies to align with your growth goals.

Our collaborative approach involves understanding your unique challenges to recommend the best funding options. We work with Family Offices, Venture Capitalists, and Private Equity Investors who provide both capital and mentorship.

We also assist in creating vital documents like Investment Memorandums, Pitch Decks and Financials to ensure your readiness to attract investors.

M&A ADVISORY

At Fuelling Rockets, our M&A Advisory service helps businesses navigate mergers and acquisitions smoothly. Whether you want to grow your market share, diversify your portfolio, or meet strategic goals, we provide expert guidance at every step.

Our experienced team understands different industries well, crafting tailored strategies to match your specific objectives. From planning and finding targets to negotiating, due diligence, and finalizing deals, we ensure a seamless process and successful outcomes.

Trust us to steer your M&A journey with precision and integrity towards achieving your growth goals.

DEBT SYNDICATION

At Fuelling Rockets, we specialize in Debt Syndication, helping businesses secure financing through structured loans and credit facilities. Whether you need funds for growth, working capital, or debt refinancing, our expert team provides tailored advisory services.

From designing the right financial package to finalizing agreements, we ensure a smooth process to enhance your financial setup and achieve your business objectives.
